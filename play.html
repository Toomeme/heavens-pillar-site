Heaven's Pillar Early Gameplay Sample

Please note in this gameplay demo, only Meihua Lin will be playable.

Default Controls

Keyboard

  • Movement: WASD
  • Punch: K
  • Kick: O
  • Special: P
  • Jump: Space
  • Pause: Enter

Gamepad

  • Movement: Directional Pad
  • Punch: X / ◻
  • Kick: Y / △
  • Special: B / 🞅
  • Jump: A / 🞩
  • Pause: Start

Controls can be freely changed from the options menu.
